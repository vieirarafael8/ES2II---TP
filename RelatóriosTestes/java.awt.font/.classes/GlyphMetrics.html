Many different glyphs can be used to represent a single 49  * character or combination of characters. {@code GlyphMetrics} 50  * instances are produced by {@link java.awt.Font Font} and are applicable 51  * to a specific glyph in a particular {@code Font}. 52  * <p> 53  * Glyphs are either STANDARD, LIGATURE, COMBINING, or COMPONENT. 54  * <ul> 55  * <li>STANDARD glyphs are commonly used to represent single characters. 56  * <li>LIGATURE glyphs are used to represent sequences of characters. 57  * <li>COMPONENT glyphs in a {@link GlyphVector} do not correspond to a 58  * particular character in a text model.
